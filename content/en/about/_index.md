### Biography

Liangbin Zhao received the B.S. degree in Communication Engineering from the School of Information and Electronics, Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT), Beijing, China, in 2019. He has been pursuing the Ph.D. degree in Information and Communication Engineering at BIT under the supervision of [Prof. Xiangyuan Bu](https://cnet.bit.edu.cn/sztd/js/b103673.htm) since 2020.  
Since 2023, he has been a visiting Ph.D. student supported by the China Scholarship Council at the School of Computing, Macquarie University, Sydney, Australia, supervised by [Dr. Yimeng Feng](https://researchers.mq.edu.au/en/persons/yimeng-feng) and [Prof. Andrew Zhang](https://profiles.uts.edu.au/Andrew.Zhang).

His current research interests include wireless communication, signal processing, and integrated sensing and communication (ISAC).
